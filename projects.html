CIV102 Bridge

Ursa

Ursa was a robust robot built for the FIRST Robotics Competition Crescendo Challenge. As Co-Captain of my team, I led the process of robot-build including strategical design, wiring, wire management, and troubleshooting. With Ursa, we qualified for District Championships and were Alliance Captains at the Windsor Competition.

Koda

Koda was a robot built for the FIRST Robotics Competition Charged Up Challenge. Through this build process, I learned how to approach iteration. Our claw broke 6 times throughout the season, but we kept building very similar iterations. If we had tried to pivot to a new design, as long as we prototyped, we likely would have been able to build a more reliable claw.

Sapphire

In 2022 at SHAD University of Prince Edward Island, our team developed a prototype and a pitch for Sapphire, a sexual education website to address the wellbeing of Canadian teens for our design project.

Callisto

Callisto was the first ever robot I ever worked on, and was built for the FIRST Robotics Competition Rapid React Challenge. As Electrical Lead I led the wiring process of the control system. With Callisto, we qualified for District Championships and World Championships in Houston, TX.
